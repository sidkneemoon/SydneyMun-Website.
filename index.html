Sydney Mun

Sustainability Studies

My name is Sydney Mun and I'm currently a 3rd year Sustainability Studies major at University of California, Riverside. I have a strong passion for Entomology, Conservation, and Ecology. My interests stem from childhood memories of collecting insects in my backyard, to going to a Zoological high school, to being able to pursue what I enjoy learning about in a college setting. Through taking various classes, such as Entm010 and BPSC021, my knowledge of what interests me has increased and allows me to utilize what I learned in a practical way.

I have worked in various lab settings, from a Cockroach lab, to an Ant Lab, to a Plant Biology Lab. Through this, I have gained skills like time management, lab maintenance, communication, and animal and plant rearing. In the Cockroach lab, I was one of three student assistants maintaining colonies and helping them with their pest repellent research. In the Ant lab, I assist with making hydrogel baits and counting ants in order to analyze them. In the Plant Biology Lab, I help in various areas, such as the lab to help with bacteria counting and sample labeling, in the greenhouse to assist in the growth of the plants and to take note of pH and carbon emission, do financial management, and I help the website and Social Media for the CAFE.

I am currently a very active member in the Christian clubs Kristos Campus Missions and Acts2Fellowship and have served in the special needs department at my home church. I have been apart of KCM since 2019 and have been apart of the Prayer Committee since 2020. I joined A2F in 2020, am a very active member, and help out for various events when I can. In the Special Needs department, I spent time one-on-one with a person with special needs and cared for them, entertained them, and did Bible Study together. I believe my upbringing as a Korean-American in a predominantly Hispanic/Latino neighborhood and caring for people with special needs, including my own brother has shaped my communication skills to understand and relate with diverse groups of people.
